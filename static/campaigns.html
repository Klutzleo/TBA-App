TBA Updates
🎉 What's New
🎯 Stat Checks New
Story Weavers can now send formal stat checks from the 📊 dashboard tab. Three modes — Character (player rolls against a hidden difficulty), Character VS NPC (contested roll, both fire simultaneously), and NPC (auto-resolves, optional hidden). Add flavor text for the whole table to see.
🖼️ Profile Photos New
You can now upload a profile picture! Head to your profile, hover your avatar and click to change it, or use the Edit Profile button. Character cards in the game sidebar also show a 👤 link to view any player's profile mid-session.
📊 Three Layers of Stats New
Stats now live at every level — your personal profile tracks lifetime rolls, attacks, callings & more across all campaigns. The campaigns page boast bar shows universe-wide totals. And the 📊 button in-game shows stats for that specific campaign.
⚡ Tether Boosts New
Tethers now work like BAP — after any roll, hit the ⚡ Boost button to apply BAP and tethers together in one epic fanfare card. Boost now appears on all roll types: stat checks, attacks, defense rolls, and /roll dice expressions. Set tethers on your character sheet just like BAP.
🗣️ Inline Rolls Mid-Narrative New
Drop a roll command at the end of any message: "I leap across the chasm... /pp" — your narration posts first, then the roll card follows. Works with /pp, /ip, /sp, /roll, /attack, /defense, /cast, /technique, and /s.
🔜 What's Next
🏆 Achievements Soon
Earn badges for milestones — first battle survived, 100 dice rolled, The Calling triggered, and more. Stats are already being tracked so achievements will unlock retroactively.
🤝 Social & Friends Soon
Follow other players, see their public profiles, and get notified when your friends start a campaign.
🎮 Discord Integration Soon
Link your Discord account to sync your TBA profile and earn the TBA Player role in the community server.
