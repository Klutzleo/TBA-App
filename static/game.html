Welcome to TBA!

Tools for the Bad Ass — a tabletop RPG built for epic stories, wild dice rolls, and people who show up ready to make something unforgettable.
Step 1 — Get into the game
👁
You're a Spectator right now. You've joined but haven't created your character yet — you're one step away from jumping in.
⚔️
Hit "+ New Character" in the sidebar. Fill in your name and stats, and you're ready to roll.
✅
Your Story Weaver may approve it first. Just hang tight — you'll be notified the moment you're in.
Step 2 — Know your stats
PP Physical Power — punching, running, lifting
IP Intellect Power — magic, tech, tactics
SP Social Power — charm, heal, support
DP Damage Points — your hit points
Edge Bonus from experience — grows with level
BAP Bad Ass Points — earned for epic moments
Step 3 — How rolling works
Attack: Roll your weapon die + your stat (PP/IP/SP) + Edge. Beat the defender's roll to deal damage.
Defend: Roll your defense die + your stat + Edge. Higher = less damage taken.
Initiative: 1d6 + PP + Edge. Highest goes first.
BAP token — awarded by the SW for bold, creative, or hilarious plays. Adds a bonus die to your next roll. Use it wisely!

You're the Story Weaver!

You're the narrator, the world-builder, the one who makes everything feel alive. Your players are counting on you — and this app is built to make that as fun as possible.
Get your session started:
🔑
Share your campaign code with players. Hit ⚙ Settings in the sidebar to copy it. Players enter it on the home screen to join.
🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Approve characters as players join. Depending on your settings, you may need to review and approve each character before they can play.
⚔️
Start combat when the story calls for it. Type /initiative start in chat to kick off an encounter. Players will roll their own initiative.
🧌
Create NPCs from the sidebar. Build villains, allies, and monsters — then speak as them directly in chat using the bubble bar.

📖 Quick Reference

Chat Formatting
**bold** → bold
*italic* → italic
_italic_ → italic
@Name → mention a player (push notification)
@narrator / @sw / @gm → ping the Story Weaver
🎲 ⚔️ ✨ Emojis work anywhere in chat
Chat Commands — Players
/ooc <message> — Out-of-character chat (goes to OOC tab)
/whisper @Player <message> — Private message
/w @Player <message> — Whisper shorthand
/who — List party members and their stats
Roles
🎲 Story Weaver
GM. Controls NPCs, encounters, and the world. Has campaign settings and full initiative control.
⚔️ Player
Has an approved character. Rolls dice, attacks, defends, and casts abilities.
👁 Spectator
Joined but hasn't created a character yet. Can watch but not act.
Dice Commands — Players
/roll 2d6+3 — Roll any dice expression
/attack @Target — Roll your attack (weapon die + PP + Edge) vs target
/pp — Physical stat check (1d6 + PP + Edge)
/ip — Intellect stat check (1d6 + IP + Edge)
/sp — Social stat check (1d6 + SP + Edge)
/defense pp|ip|sp — Manual defense roll (defense die + stat + Edge)
/initiative — Roll initiative (1d6 + PP + Edge)
/initiative show — Display current initiative order
/ability — Use an ability macro (e.g. /fireball)
/ability @Target — Use ability on a specific target
Commands — Story Weaver
/s XdY — Secret roll — result visible to SW only
/initiative start — Start encounter without rolling
/initiative @Name — Roll initiative for a PC or NPC
/initiative silent @Name — Hidden roll, only SW sees result
/initiative next — Advance to the next turn
/initiative end — End encounter & restore all ability uses
/initiative clear — Clear rolls without ending encounter
/rest — Short rest — restore ability uses only
/env [tier] @Target [source] — Environmental attack (tier 1–5)
/scene [title] — Post a scene transition card and save to Lore
Stats
PP — Physical Power
Adds to attack rolls and physical initiative.
IP — Intellect Power
Used for magic/technique rolls. Initiative tiebreaker #2.
SP — Social Power
Used for healing and support. Initiative tiebreaker #3.
DP — Damage Points
Your hit points. Reaches 0 = trigger The Calling.
Edge
Adds to initiative roll and special bonuses. Grows with level.
BAP — Bad Ass Points
Granted by SW for creative play. Adds a bonus die to your next roll.
Initiative
Formula: 1d6 + PP + Edge
Tiebreaker order: PP → IP → SP
Ability uses reset at end of encounter.
💀 The Calling
Triggered when DP reaches 0. SW asks a threshold question.
Outcomes: Survive · Scarred (gains "The Called" status) · Dead
5th Calling = permanent death. Characters with 4 Callings are on the edge.

The Called — A scarred character is haunted between sessions. SW runs a Called Check (⚙ menu → Called Check) before their next rest: roll 1d6 — 1-2 nightmare (−1 next roll), 3-5 peaceful, 6 vision (+1 next roll). SW can cleanse the status via ritual at any time.

Memory Echo — When a character dies in The Calling, their allies may be driven to avenge them. This is story-defined — the SW applies the bonus manually using Active Effects (✨ on any character card). What counts as "avenging" depends on the narrative: a creature, a faction, a moment. Set the modifier and duration to match the story.

Using Brave?

Brave blocks push notifications by default. To enable:
1. Go to brave://settings/privacy
2. Turn on "Use Google services for push messaging"
3. Restart Brave, then come back and enable notifications.
